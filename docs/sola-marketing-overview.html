What is Sola?
Sola is a mobile app for women who travel solo. It gives them destination guides, trip planning tools, safety features, and a community of other women travelers — all in one place.
The one-liner
Sola is the first travel app designed specifically for women traveling alone.
Who is it for?
Women aged 25–45 who travel solo or are thinking about it. They're independent, curious, and value quality information over generic travel content. They want to feel prepared, not scared.
TraitDescription
Age range25–45, skewing millennial and early Gen Z professionals
Travel styleSolo or semi-solo (occasionally with one friend). Not group tours.
MindsetConfident but practical. Wants honest info, not sugar-coated marketing.
Pain pointSafety info is scattered across Reddit, blogs, and Facebook groups. No single source of truth.
What she valuesAuthenticity, structured information, community with real women — not influencers.
What does the app actually do?
The app has five main areas. Think of them as five tabs at the bottom of the screen.
Home
Your personal dashboard. Shows your trips, saved places, recommendations, and safety shortcuts.
Discover
Browse countries, cities, activities, and curated collections. This is where all the destination content lives.
Community
Discussions with other women travelers + a feed of traveler profiles you can connect and message with.
Trips
Create and manage your trips. Includes a travel journal, saved places, safety tools, and traveler matching.
Profile
Your identity in the app. Interests, visited countries, settings, notifications, and identity verification.
SOLA Product Playbook — 2
Feature Area 1
Discover
This is the biggest part of the app. It's where users research destinations. All content is written and structured with solo women travelers in mind.
Country Guides
Rich editorial pages for 40+ countries. Each one includes: a solo-friendliness rating, budget breakdown (accommodation, food, transport, activities), best months to visit, experiences, cities, transport and health info, social scene recommendations, and real questions from the community.
City Guides
Three sections per city: an overview with neighborhoods, a filterable list of places (hotels, restaurants, cafes, attractions), and a calendar of local events. Users can save any place to their personal list.
Experiences
Individual pages for tours, wellness retreats, landmarks, and activities. Each one has key info at a glance (duration, price, difficulty), tips from women travelers, practical details like address and booking links, and tags for filtering.
Curated Collections
Themed lists like "Beach towns for first-timers" or "Best cities for solo nightlife." Editorially curated, visually presented.
Search
Search across all destinations, cities, countries, and activities. Includes recent searches, popular destinations, and quick suggestion chips.

Feature Area 2
Trips
Users can create, manage, and document their trips. Safety tools are built directly into the trip experience — not hidden in a settings menu.
Creating a trip
Users walk through a simple flow:
  1. Choose trip type
    Planning a future trip, currently traveling, or logging a past trip
  2. Add destinations
    Search and add up to 5 cities or countries as stops
  3. Set dates
    Pick start and end dates — the app calculates the number of nights
  4. Customize
    Add a trip name, cover photo, and invite travel companions from your connections
Inside a trip — three tabs
Journey
A travel journal. Add notes, photos, and highlights as you go. This becomes your trip memory.
Plan
Your safety command center. Emergency numbers, embassy lookup, share-my-trip, emergency contact, and your saved places for this destination.
People
See other women whose trips overlap with yours by city and date. Opt-in, with full privacy controls.
Feature Area 3
Safety Tools
These are spread across the app but most are accessible from the trip Plan tab. Safety is not a separate section — it's part of how the app works.
Emergency Numbers
Country-specific police, ambulance, and fire numbers for 40+ countries. One tap to call directly from the app.
Share My Trip
Sends a pre-written message with your destination and dates to a trusted contact. One tap, via the phone's native share sheet.
Emergency Contact
Save a trusted person's name and phone number inside the app. The home screen reminds you to set one up before you travel.
Embassy Lookup
A direct link to your country's embassy directory for wherever you're traveling. One tap from the trip dashboard.
Identity Verification
Users verify their identity with a selfie and live pose prompt. Verified badge appears on their profile. Keeps the community trustworthy.
Safety Signals
Every country guide includes honest solo-friendliness ratings, transport safety, health info, and cultural context — not sugar-coated marketing.

Feature Area 4
Community
A women-only space to ask real questions, get honest answers, and find other solo travelers. Two sections: Discussions and Travelers.
Discussions
A Q&A feed where women ask and answer travel questions.
  • Users post questions tagged by topic and destination (e.g. "Safety in Medellín" or "Best hostels in Lisbon")
  • Other women reply with their experiences and advice
  • Sola Team posts are clearly marked with a badge — these are editorial or conversation-starting questions from us
  • Users can upvote helpful threads
  • First-time visitors see an intro banner explaining the community
  • First-time posters get guided placeholder text to help them write a good question
Travelers
A feed of other women on the platform. Users can browse, connect, and message.
  • Traveler profiles — name, photo, home country, bio, interests, visited countries
  • Connection requests — send a request to connect. Both sides must accept before messaging.
  • Matching — the app suggests women with similar interests or overlapping travel plans
  • Direct messaging — real-time private chat with connected women. Block and report built in.
Feature Area 5
Home Feed
A personalized dashboard that adapts to each user. It's the first thing they see when they open the app.
Trip Status Card
Changes based on the user's situation: prompts to plan a trip if none exists, shows a countdown for upcoming trips, or shows current trip info when traveling.
Personalized Recommendations
City suggestions based on the user's stated interests (food, nightlife, outdoors, etc.) and browsing history.
Saved Places
A preview of places the user has bookmarked while browsing. Quick access to their shortlist.
Community Highlights
A preview of recent discussion threads, so users stay connected even from the home screen.
Safety Shortcuts
Quick links to safety tools, emergency contact setup, and trip info — always one tap away.
Vibe Sections
Themed content blocks like "Slow travel" or "City escapes" — two different ones shown each session to keep the feed fresh.
SOLA Product Playbook — 5
Feature Area 6
Onboarding & Profile
How users sign up and set up their identity in the app.
Sign-up flow
Users sign up with Google or email. Then they walk through a short onboarding flow:
  1. Travel intent
    "I've got a trip coming up" vs. "Just dreaming for now" — this personalizes the entire app experience from the start
  2. Interests and priorities
    Pick what they care about: food, outdoors, nightlife, history, wellness, etc. Plus priorities like safety, meeting locals, hidden gems, solo-friendly spaces
  3. Next trip (optional)
    Enter a destination and dates so the home feed is immediately relevant
  4. Profile setup
    Name, photo, home country, bio, username, and countries visited
Profile features
  • Profile photo, name, username, home country flag, bio
  • Interests displayed as tags (up to 8 categories)
  • Visited countries shown as a flag list — editable at any time
  • Saved places count and trip history visible on profile
  • Identity verification status (unverified / pending / verified)
  • Notification feed — connection requests, replies, upvotes, system alerts
  • Settings: currency, language, emergency contact, privacy, sign out, delete account

The solo travel app for women. Destination guides, trip planning, safety tools, and a community — all in one place.

Sola is a mobile app that gives women everything they need to travel solo with confidence. We combine destination intelligence — country guides, city guides, curated experiences — with built-in safety tools and a verified women-only community.
